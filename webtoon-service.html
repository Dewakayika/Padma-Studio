our process

Our process begins with understanding your vision and storyboard, ensuring every detail aligns with your narrative. We then use advanced 3D modeling techniques to create immersive backgrounds, bringing your webtoon to life

01

Planning

This initial step involves understanding the story and its flow through detailed consultations with the client. A storyboard is created to visualize the narrative, ensuring key scenes and transitions are clearly outlined. Checkpoints for 3D assets are established, identifying all necessary elements and setting timelines for their creation and approval.

02

Background Design

In this phase, backgrounds are aligned with the storyboard using a 3D application. The design process includes multiple layers such as line art, texture, and shadows to create a rich and detailed environment. Maintaining accurate perspective to match the storyboard is crucial for consistency and visual coherence.

03

Finishing

The backgrounds created in the 3D application are then imported into Adobe Photoshop. Here, they are refined and integrated into the overall artwork, ensuring they seamlessly blend with characters and other elements. This step focuses on polishing the visuals and adding final touches to enhance the overall aesthetic.

04

Quality Control

This final step involves a thorough review of the webtoon before its launch. The team checks for consistency, accuracy, and overall quality, making any necessary adjustments. This ensures the final product meets the client’s expectations and is ready for publication.

Furawashi

Project by ViviOn Japan

What we do:

In this project, PADMA Studio collaborated on the process of replacing the background for the webtoon titled Furawashi. The scope of work included designing the angles and perspectives of 3D assets provided by the client. The PADMA Studio team worked to ensure that the backgrounds aligned with the storyline and the perspective of the storyboard images. We handled the line art, base textures, and shadows on different layers as needed for the job.

In the next step, we imported each layer into Photoshop files, which were then arranged according to the story and storyboard.Content updates and copywriting improvements were also part of the project.

Each layer was meticulously crafted to ensure it complemented the webtoon’s aesthetic and storyline.
